My name is Ann Layman and I'm a fast-paced, forward-thinker. I was born and raised in Seattle but I have been around the globe and I currently live in New York City. I like maps, Gose beer and JavaScript. After graduating from the University of Washington where I studied Geographic Information Systems, I followed my love for travel and worked for two years as a travel advisor for two travel agencies in Seattle. To challenge myself and to continue learning, I later moved to Madrid, Spain for two years where I taught English to children and adults alike. There, I spent my freetime traveling throughout Europe, becoming fluent in Spanish and also focusing on my goal of becoming a web developer. I recently graduated from the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy- a full-time Javascript Coding Bootcamp For Women. There I learned ES6, Node.js, Express, React and Redux and applied my skills by developing several projects both as a member of a team and independently. Now as I begin my job search I spend my time also managing my startup HopPlotter,the world's best brewery-finding application, and playing the alto saxophone.
If you have any questions, want to work on a project together, or you'd like to say hello, you can contact me at ann@hopplotter.com
